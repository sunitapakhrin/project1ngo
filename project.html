PROJECTS

Helping Hand Aid has had the opportunity to serve humanity around the globe since 1999. Whether supporting victims of natural disasters or civil conflicts, our volunteers have been on hand to provide aid. None of this would be possible without the support of the sangat (community), who provided social, psychological, spiritual, and financial support.
